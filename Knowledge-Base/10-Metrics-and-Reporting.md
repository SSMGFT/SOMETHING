
# 10 - Metrics and Reporting

> Version: 1.2

# Learning Objectives

After this module you should be able to:

- Explain why security metrics matter.
- Distinguish metrics from reports.
- Understand KPIs and KRIs.
- Present technical findings in business terms.

---

# Why Metrics Matter

Security teams collect technical data.

Management needs business information.

```text
Technical Findings
        │
        ▼
Metrics
        │
        ▼
Reports
        │
        ▼
Management Decisions
```

Metrics transform evidence into decisions.

---

# Characteristics of Good Metrics

Good metrics are:

- Relevant
- Measurable
- Repeatable
- Timely
- Actionable

Avoid collecting data that cannot influence decisions.

---

# KPI vs KRI

| KPI (Key Performance Indicator) | KRI (Key Risk Indicator) |
|---------------------------------|--------------------------|
| Measures performance | Measures risk exposure |
| "How well are we doing?" | "How much risk are we carrying?" |
| Operational focus | Risk focus |

Examples:

| KPI | KRI |
|-----|-----|
| Patch compliance % | Critical unpatched systems |
| Mean time to remediate | Number of exploitable vulnerabilities |
| Security awareness completion | Phishing click rate |

---

# Reporting Levels

| Audience | Report Content |
|----------|----------------|
| Executives | Risk, trends, business impact |
| Managers | Status, priorities, remediation |
| Technical Teams | Detailed findings and evidence |

Always tailor the report to the audience.

---

# Effective Security Reports

A report should answer:

1. What happened?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What is the business impact?
4. What action is recommended?
5. What is the current risk?

---

# Dashboards

Good dashboards highlight:

- Trends
- Exceptions
- High-risk items
- Progress over time

Dashboards support decisions; they are not data dumps.

---

# CISSP Exam Traps

| If the question says... | Think... |
|-------------------------|----------|
| Executive audience | Business impact |
| Board reporting | Trends and risk |
| Technical remediation | Detailed findings |
| Measure performance | KPI |
| Measure risk | KRI |
